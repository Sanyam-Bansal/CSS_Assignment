Different Heights of Lines!

Height: 5px
Pixels(px) are relative to the viewing devices
Height: 5em
Relative to the font-size of the element
Height: 5rem
Relative to the font-size of the root element
Height: 5vw
Relative to the 1% of the width of the viewport
Height: 5vh
Relative to the 1% of the height of the viewport
